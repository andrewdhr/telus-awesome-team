The TELUS Awesome Team deliver customer centric insights and drive digital transformation through the use of advanced analytics and AI/ML techniques. There are three sub-team focuses within in the team that working closely together to achieve team objectives: The Data Science sub-team leverages machine learning principles and data science techniques and work on projects that potentially offer Opex savings and/or add incremental revenues. The Geoanalytics sub-team use and maintain the PDC platform for setting up geofenced marketing campaigns. The Telemetry sub-team perform insights on current data science and/or PDC projects and drive the RPA (Robotic Process Automation) and Data Engineering process.
